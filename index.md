I am currently pursuing a PhD in mathematics at [Laboratoire des Signaux et Systèmes (L2S)](https://l2s.centralesupelec.fr) in Paris-Saclay, France, under the supervision of [Jean Auriol](https://l2s.centralesupelec.fr/u/auriol-jean/) and [Lucas Brivadis](https://sites.google.com/view/lucas-brivadis/homepage?authuser=0).
My research focuses on control theory, partial differential equations, and time-delay systems.
More precisely, I am working on robust stabilization of networks of interconnected hyperbolic PDEs and observability of ODE-PDE cascades.
